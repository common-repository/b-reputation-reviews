== Description ==

Collect and display your clients' reviews and testimonials and transform them into Google stars.
[B-Reputation.com](https://b-reputation.com) is France's first B2B review website, enabling companies to create their own surveys, adapt them to each client, and immediately showcase the results on the Internet.
With this plugin, companies can automatically display all their latest reviews and ask customers to leave one themselves.



== Installation ==

    Upload the entire B-Reputation-Reviews folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
    Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'B-Reputation Settings' under 'Settings' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
Enter your company Siren and name in settings fields to load reviews from [B-Reputation.com](https://b-reputation.com).


Now, copy the following shortcode into pages or posts:
`[b_reputation_display]`
